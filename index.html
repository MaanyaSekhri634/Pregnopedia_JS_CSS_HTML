Every Pregnancy Matters

Pregnopedia is pregnant women-centric health website providing awareness about balanced diet and physical exercise regimen. Many pregnancy complications can be prevented by looking after the health of expectant mothers.

Learn More

Why is it Important that
You take care?

  1. 01

    According to a recent report by the National Institute of Health(NIH) the Maternal Mortality Rate is 99/1 lakh live births.

  2. 02

    Lack of physical exercise during pregnancy results in higher rate of caesarean section, as normal delivery becomes difficult.

  3. 03

    Lack of balanced diet during pregnancy may lead to woman being anaemic and fetal growth retardation.

  4. 04

    Balanced diet and physical exercise helps a woman alleviate anxiety and depression which is common during pregnancy.
