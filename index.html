Every Pregnancy Matters

Pregnopedia is a pregnancy-focused health website that promotes awareness of a balanced diet and physical exercise regimen to prevent pregnancy complications.

Learn More

Why is it Important that
You take care?

  1. 01

    Did you know that the Maternal Mortality Rate in the US is 99 deaths per 100,000 live births? This alarming statistic was revealed in a recent report by the National Institute of Health (NIH). We need to be aware of these numbers so that we can work towards improving the health and safety of mothers and babies across the country.

  2. 02

    Did you know that staying active during pregnancy can help reduce the likelihood of having a caesarean section? Studies show that lack of physical activity during pregnancy can make normal delivery more difficult, leading to an increased chance of needing a c-section. So why not take a walk, do some prenatal yoga, or try some lightweight training to help keep you and your baby healthy?

  3. 03

    Did you know that not having a balanced diet during pregnancy can have serious consequences for both the mother and the baby? It can lead to anaemia in the mother and even affect the growth of the baby. It's important to make sure you're eating a healthy and well-rounded diet to ensure a healthy pregnancy and a healthy baby.

  4. 04

    Did you know that maintaining a balanced diet and engaging in physical exercise can work wonders in alleviating anxiety and depression in pregnant women? It's a common issue that many expecting mothers face, and taking care of your mind and body can make a huge difference. So why not try incorporating some healthy habits into your routine today?
